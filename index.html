How It Works

Follow these simple steps with Noshahi Test Builder to create and export your custom test paper.

1

Set School Profile

Add your school's name and upload your logo to enable branded PDF exports.

2

Browse Question Bank

Filter questions by class (9–12), subject, chapter, and topic to find what you need.

3

Select Questions

Pick questions manually or by topic and chapter coverage to assemble your test.

4

Add Custom Questions

Teachers can add their own custom questions directly to the question bank.

5

Export as PDF

Generate a print-ready, branded PDF test paper with one click — no internet needed to print.

6

Print & Distribute

Print the paper test and distribute to students for offline use. No online grading required.
