Enhanced Features

The complete toolkit for 9-12 education test management

Syllabus-Aligned Question Bank

A single place where all questions are stored and organized by Class, Subject, Chapter, and Topic. It is specially designed for Matric and Intermediate students, so every question matches your syllabus and teaching plan.

Custom Exam Patterns

Take full control of your tests. You can choose chapters, set difficulty levels, and follow board patterns. Teachers can also easily add their own questions to the question bank.

Branded PDF Export

Generate professional, print-ready PDFs in seconds. Every test comes pre-formatted with your school's official branding, including logo and name, for a consistent institutional look.

Multi-Role Management

Give your whole institution easy access with different roles. Admins can manage staff, teachers can create tests, and students can check their test schedules.
